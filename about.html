About us

Here at Cool AF Recipes Co.™, we believe that anybody can be cooks with simple to use recipes.

We strive to create easy to read and learn recipes for anyone with an interest in cooking for themselves or others.

Meet the founders

Cool AF Recipes Co.™ is created and founded by Mr. and Mrs. Thompson, a couple who’s been in the cooking scene since 1984.

This lovely couple has tasted and reviewed every recipe on the website, making sure the quality’s all top notch and ready to be made by you in your own kitchen.
